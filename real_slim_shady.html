My Approach

My approach has always been fairly simple: the client (or clients) and I identify the full extent of the presenting problem and work together toward a practical solution. To do this I draw from my knowledge of brain function, cognitive behavioral psychology, positive psychology and an eclectic sampling of other theoretical points of view that can shed light on what’s happening in the client’s world. I’m respectful, I engage with the client and I like to use humor, imagery and metaphors in my work.

About Me

I’ve been helping couples, individuals and families address life issues since 1983. I’ve been happily married to the same guy since 1969, enjoy my children, grandchildren and friends and have two other professions: I research and write books about Napa Valley history, and I’m an artist (see www.linweber.com ).
